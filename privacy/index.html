Privacy

Privacy Policy

Your creativity belongs to you. Pixel Beader keeps your creative work private and local.

No Personal Data Collection

Pixel Beader does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or user tracking services.

Offline & Local Processing

All data processing, including converting photos into pixel art, editing bead grids, and rendering simulated fused beads in 3D, is performed entirely on your device. Your photos and designs are never uploaded to any server or cloud services.

In-App Purchases

Any transactions or subscriptions made within the app are processed securely through Apple's App Store payment systems. Pixel Beader does not have access to your payment information or credit card details.
