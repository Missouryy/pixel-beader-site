Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Overview

Pixel Beader is designed to keep your creative work on your device. The app does not collect personal data, track users, or sell user information.

Photos and camera

Pixel Beader may request access to your photo library to import images and save exported pattern images or 3D snapshots. The app may request camera access for AR inspection. Photo and camera content is processed on device.

Purchases

Membership purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Pixel Beader uses Apple StoreKit to check purchase status and restore purchases.

Local storage

Projects, palettes, thumbnails, app preferences, and purchase entitlement state may be stored locally on your device using Apple system storage.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact missouryy@icloud.com.
