Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?
If you have upgraded your app or changed devices, open the settings menu inside Pixel Beader and tap the "Restore Purchases" button. Ensure you are logged into the same Apple Account used to make the purchase.

Where are exported snapshots saved?
Snapshots and 3D preview screenshots are saved directly to your device's photo library. You will need to grant Pixel Beader permission to write to Photos when prompted.

Are my images uploaded to any servers?
No. Pixel Beader operates entirely offline. The photo-to-bead-art conversion and 3D Metal renders are computed locally on your iPad or iPhone, ensuring complete privacy.
